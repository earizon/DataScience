# Keras 

- Keras 3 is a multi-backend deep learning framework, with support for JAX, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and 
OpenVINO (for inference-only). [[doc_has.comparative]]

- Effortlessly build and train models for computer vision, natural language processing, audio processing,
timeseries forecasting, recommender systems, etc.

- ... By picking the backend that is the fastest for your model architecture (often JAX!), leverage 
speedups ranging from 20% to 350% compared to other frameworks. Benchmark here.
    Datacenter-scale training: Scale confidently from your laptop to large clusters of GPUs or TPUs.
